# Privacy Policy for Ground Station

**Effective Date:** 15 December 2025

Ground Station (“the app”) is a personal productivity application. This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled when you use the app.

---

## Data Collection

Ground Station **does not collect any user data**.

This includes, but is not limited to:

* Personal information
* Usage or analytics data
* Device or hardware information
* Location data
* Contacts, media, or files
* Identifiers such as cookies, advertising IDs, or IP addresses

---

## Data Usage

Since no data is collected, **no data is used for any purpose**, including:

* Analytics
* Advertising
* Personalization
* App functionality tracking
* Diagnostics or crash reporting

---

## Data Sharing

Ground Station **does not share any data with third parties**.

The app does not integrate with:

* Advertising SDKs
* Analytics services
* Cloud services
* Social media platforms
* Crash reporting tools

---

## Data Storage and Security

All content created within the app is stored **locally on the user’s device only**.

* No data is transmitted off the device
* No backend servers are used
* The developer has no access to user data

Because no data leaves the device, there is no external data storage or processing.

---

## User Controls

Users have full control over their data through their device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored app data.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Ground Station is safe for all age groups.
The app **does not collect data from children or adults**, including users under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect app changes or policy requirements. Updates will be published in this document with a revised effective date.

---

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the public GitHub repository where this policy is hosted.
